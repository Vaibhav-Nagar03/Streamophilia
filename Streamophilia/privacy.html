Privacy Policy

Last updated: development build — placeholder terms for this project, not reviewed by a lawyer.

1. What We Collect

We collect your username, email, and hashed password when you sign up, plus content you create (posts, tournaments) and basic usage data like login times.

2. How We Use It

Your data is used to run your account, show your content to other users, and let admins moderate the platform. We don't sell your data to third parties.

3. Storage & Security

Your data is stored in this project's own database. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text. This is a development project, not a production-hardened system — don't use a password here that you use anywhere important.

4. Your Choices

You can request account deletion at any time, which removes your account, posts, and tournaments from the database.

5. Changes

This policy may change as the platform develops. Continued use after a change means you accept the updated policy.
